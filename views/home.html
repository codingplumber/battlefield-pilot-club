Mission Statement:

To serve by furthering our humanitarian efforts through charitable, educational and research programs in communities throughout the world.

Our Focus:

The service focus of Pilot International is helping people affected by brain-related disorders and disabilities through volunteer activities, education, and financial support.

Our History:

Pilot International was chartered on October 18, 1921 in Macon, Georgia, U.S.A. by Elizabeth Leonard and forty local businesswomen. The name “Pilot” was inspired by the mighty riverboat pilots of that day who represented leadership and guidance.
